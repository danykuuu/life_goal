Faults I notice with humanity's future:

  • In the short term, people lack care for the future because its a point in time where their current self doesn't exist. Often sacrificing their own future for the present.
  • In the long term, Earth will run out of resources. We will need to become renewable or outsource.
  • In the short term, the entire education needs to be heavily revised.
  • In the long term, humanity should consider uniting under some baseline rules.
  • However time corrupts all and a good structure will corrode if not well maintained.
